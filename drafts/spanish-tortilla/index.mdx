### Servings: 2


### Ingredients:

- 2 medium sized potatoes, cut in to half coins  around 1cm thick
- 1 onion, julienne
- 2 cloves of garlic, pressed
- canola oil
- half a teaspoon of salt 
- half a teaspoon of pepper


### Method:

1. Heat the oil to medium heat on pan. Add potatoes, fry them for 15min on low-medium heat. Stir from time to time.
2. Add onion to potatoes, and cook it for next 5 minutes.
3. In a bowl, break the eggs add garlic. The add a pinch of salt and pepper. 
4. Add egg mixture to potatoes and onion for 5 minutes (or when it will be stiff enough to flip it. I used a plate for it). Keep it under the lid on medium heat. 
5. Flip the torilla and keep it under the lid on midium for next 5 min.
6. Check if everything is according to your preferences and enjoy. It may be difficult to do the dish at the first time 
since it depend on temperature of the stove and timing. Practice makes perfect.
