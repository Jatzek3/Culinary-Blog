Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- 2 cloves of garlic, sliced
- 1 medium-size onion, diced
- 500g of carrots, sliced thickly
- 300g of potatoes, cut into smaller parts
- 500ml of boiling water
- 250ml milk 

Spices
- 1 chicken/vegetable stock cube
- salt
- 1 teaspoon of turmeric
- 1/2 teaspoon of curry spice

1. Put the olive oil into the pot, heat it to medium. Put the onion and garlic in the pot. Turn
the temp to low, and cook them for 5-7 min till the onion shrink.
2. Add the rest of the vegetables and boiling water so water will cover the vegetables.
3. Add all of the spices. Stir for a while and cook the soup for about 20 min, until the potatoes soften.
4. Turn out the gas and let it rest 
5. Add milk and mix the cream.
6. Taste and add spices according to taste(there probably will be too little salt)
