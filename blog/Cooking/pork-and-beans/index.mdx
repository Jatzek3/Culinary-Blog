### Servings: 4

### Ingredients:
- 250g of white beans
- 200g of sausage - split in half, then sliced
- 100g of pork - 1-2 cm diced
- 2 medium-sized onions - cut into 1/8 parts, through the root - flakes
- 2 tablespoons of tomato puree 
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- 1 teaspoon of pepper
- 1 teaspoon of sugar

### Method:
1. The day before put the beans into the water
2. Before the cooking, pour the water in which the beans were soaking. Then rinse them.
3. Fill the pot with beans. Add the water until it covers the beans with a little reserve.
4. Bring the beans with water to a boiling point. Turn the heat to 100 degrees Celsius. Cook the beans for ninety minutes.
5. On a skillet, on medium heat, fry the pork for 5 min.
6. Add sausage, and fry it with pork for another 5 min.
7. Add Onion, and fry with pork and sausage for another 5 min.
8. Add tomato puree and contents of the skillet to the pot with beans. Cook for 30 min.
9. Add salt, pepper, and sugar. Check the taste. Cook for the last 15 min.


### Footnotes:
- You can add bay leaves and allspice, at the time when you mix the contents of the skillet and tomato puree with beans.
- You can add marjoram and savory when you add the spices.
