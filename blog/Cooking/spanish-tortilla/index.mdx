### Coooking time: 30min

### Difficulty: Easy

### Servings: 2


### Ingredients:

- 2 medium-sized potatoes, cut into half coins around 1cm thick
- 1 onion, julienne
- 2 cloves of garlic, pressed
- canola oil
- half a teaspoon of salt 
- half a teaspoon of pepper


### Method:

1. Heat the oil to medium heat on the pan. Add potatoes, and fry them for 15min on medium heat. Stir from time to time.
2. Add onion to potatoes, and cook it for the next 5 minutes.
3. In a bowl, break the eggs and add garlic. Add  salt and pepper. 
4. Add egg mixture to potatoes and onion for 5 minutes (or when it will be stiff enough to flip it). Keep it under the lid on medium heat. 
5. Flip the tortilla and keep it under the lid on medium for the next 5 min.
6. Check if everything is according to your preferences and enjoy. It could be difficult to do the dish for the first time. 
It depends on the temperature of the stove and timing. Practice makes perfect.
