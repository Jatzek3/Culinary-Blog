### Servings: 6


### Ingredients:
- 1 cup(250g) of split peas
- 1,25l of water 
- 250g of sausage
- 3 medium-size potatoes
- 2 medium-sized onions 
- 4 allspice balls
- 2 bay leafs
- 2 garlic cloves

### Spices
- salt, pepper
- 1 beef stock cube
- 1 pinch of whole cumin


### Method:
1. Soak peas overnight. Before cooking, rinse them thoroughly.
2. Cut the potatoes into 1-2cm cubes. 
3. Add together potatoes, water, peas in a pot. Add all spices. 
Heat the Pot to a boiling point, then turn the heat to boiling point and cook for 30 min.
4. This is a good moment to dice the onion and sausage. Slice the garlic.
5. Add the diced ingredients to the soup, and cook for another 45 min, on 100 degrees celsius under a lid. Soup will be ready when the peas dissolve.
6. Add spices to taste and enjoy!

### Footnotes:
- You don't have to add sausage to the soup. It will be vegan then.
