If you have some aging vegetables in your fridge or are
a vegan, then this could be quite a good solution for your meal. 
The recipe is easy and fast, so you can do the whole dish in under 15 min. 
The dish is good but not fantastic.

### Ingredients
- 2tbsp olive oil + some more if needed
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 5 garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes chopped
- bell pepper
- salt 
- pepper
- 500g of spaghetti

### Method: 

1. In a large skillet, heat the 2 tablespoons of olive oil. 
Add the onion, garlic, and bell pepper, season with salt and pepper, and cook over moderate heat, 
stirring occasionally, until the pepper is tender, about 10 minutes.
2. Meanwhile, in a pot of salted boiling water, cook the pasta until al dente.
Drain, reserving 1 cup of pasta water
3. Add the spaghetti, pasta water, tomatoes to the skillet and toss over moderate 
heat for 1 minute, then serve
