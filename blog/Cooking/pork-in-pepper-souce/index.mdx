### Servings: 2

I ate one time a simmilar dish when i was visiting London. Didn't expect it to be that good, but it was really well prepered. I wanted to recreate that recipe, and I managed to do it to certain degree.

### Ingredients:
 
200 grams of rice
200 grams of pork (ham preferably)
200 grams of onions cut in half and sliced


### Souce:

4 tablespoons of sauce souce
2 tablespoons of fish sauce
2 teaspoons of of ground pepper

### Method:
1. Make rice
2. Cut the pork int 2cm stripes/pieces
4. Put all ingredients on a pan and cook them for about 15-20 minutes
