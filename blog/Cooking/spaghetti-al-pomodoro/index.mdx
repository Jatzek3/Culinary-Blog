### Servings: 2


### Ingredients:
- 250g of spaghetti pasta
- 1 can(400g) of diced tomatoes
- 3 tablespoons(50ml) of olive oil
- 3 garlic cloves, finely sliced
- 1/2 medium onion, finely diced
- salt, pepper

### Method:
1. Pour olive oil on the pan, on medium heat. Cook garlic and onion on the pan for about 6 minutes.
2. Boil water in a medium-sized pot. Add spaghetti pasta to salted water. Cook until 
pasta is <em>al dente</em>.
3. Add canned tomatoes, salt, and pepper to the sauce. Cook for 5-10 minutes.
4. Add pasta to the pan. Stir for about 3 minutes.

Enjoy your meal!

### Footnotes:
- You can add basil leaves, at the end of cooking. 
- You can also sprinkle some Parmesan on top of the dish.
- If your sauce is too thick, you can add the pasta water.
