### Servings: 2



### Ingredients:

200 grams of rice
200 grams of pork (ham preferably)
200 grams of onions


### Souce:

4 tablespoons of sauce Souce
2 tablespoons of fish sauce
2 teaspoons of of ground pepper

### Method:
1. Make rice
2. Mix all other ingredients and cook them for about 20 minutes.
